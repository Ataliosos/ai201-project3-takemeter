Before finalizing the taxonomy, I reviewed approximately 35 posts from Reddit career and IT communities. The most common patterns were users asking for advice, sharing personal job search experiences, providing actionable guidance, and posting strong opinions without evidence. These observations motivated the final label taxonomy.

## Dataset Target

I will collect and label 240 examples:
- 60 actionable_advice
- 60 personal_experience
- 60 hot_take
- 60 question_request

This balanced distribution prevents any single label from exceeding 70% of the dataset and supports fair model evaluation.
